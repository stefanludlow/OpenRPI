Used only in strike.

2^14 = 16384 gives us the best precision for our range of hp and skill. The integer math matches the correctly rounded value about 49% of the time as opposed to casting the value from floats (which are the same 51% of the time). Accurate within 1% of the skill value and about 1/20th the speed of "nearestint" and about 5/6th the speed of casting floats.
